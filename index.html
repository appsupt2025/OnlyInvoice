Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first invoice?

Simply tap the '+' button in the app, select 'New Invoice', and follow our step-by-step guided process. You can customize the invoice number, add items, and set payment terms all in one place.

Can I customize my invoice template?

Yes! OnlyInvoice offers multiple professional templates that you can customize with your business logo, colors, and preferred layout.

How do I manage multiple currencies?

OnlyInvoice supports multiple currencies. You can select your preferred currency when creating a new invoice or set a default currency in the app settings.

Can I track payments?

Yes, OnlyInvoice includes comprehensive payment tracking. Mark invoices as paid, partially paid, or overdue, and get instant visibility into your business's cash flow.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. All your business and client information is stored securely on your device, and you can back up your data to your preferred cloud storage service.
